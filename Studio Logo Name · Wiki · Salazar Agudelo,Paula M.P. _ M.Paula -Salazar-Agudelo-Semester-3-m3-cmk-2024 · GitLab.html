    Name For our group

    • Brainstorming logo name
    • Example of the process

    Introduction

    During our team brainstorming sessions, we set out to choose a name that reflected our studio’s identity and vision. We didn’t have a clear idea, so we decided to separately search for names for our studio. We used a user-centered design approach, focusing on UX design principles to guide our discussions. This method involved gathering information, fostering collaboration, and exploring various creative ideas with the name and also the logo. Taking into account feedback from our professor, we embarked on a dynamic process to finalize the name selection.

    All Name Ideas Together

    In our initial brainstorming session, we started by going through several name options. This image captures some of the first ideas written on sticky notes, including “Pixel Peak” and “Pixel Paranoia.” Pixel Peak was one of my ideas that we could bring to our studio.\\\\\\\_

    Second Round of Choosing Name

    In this image, the team explored different names over the course of a few days. Several options were placed on the board, demonstrating a collaborative effort to find the perfect name for our studio. Here you can see names like "Creature Creations" and "HexaBeetle." Also, because we couldn't come up with a name that we all liked, we decided to do it by voting twice for the names that we liked the most to represent our studio.

    Final Decision Name

    The final image shows the outcome of our brainstorming: "Pixel Paranoia." After weighing the options, the team agreed that this name perfectly encapsulated our vision for creativity and innovation.

    Final Logo Concept

    The logo for our studio is the final decision made by our team, representing our definitive logo. We received valuable feedback from our professors regarding our design choices. They emphasized the importance of basing our logo on UX/UI design principles, which helped us determine that this logo is the most appropriate for our studio. Each member presented their individual logo ideas, showcasing diverse concepts, and collectively we chose this design for its creativity and alignment with our studio's vision.
